Notes

  • Effective FTE - This is the full-time equivalent teaching load for an individual. It is considered "effective" because it is calculated from their contract FTE (e.g. part-time, full-time, etc), and any additional buyouts (e.g. from grants). The baseline for all calculation is an expectation of 1725 hours per year for a staff member on 1.0 FTE.

  • Research - This is the research contribution (i.e. the ratio of their time spent on research activities) for a given staff member, including supervision responsibilities.

  • Admin - This is the admin contribution for an individual (i.e. the ratio of their time spent on specific administrative responsibilities). For most staff, this figure is expected to be zero. Those with recognised administrative roles should see them included here.

  • Teaching Hours - This is the estimated number of hours the given staff member is expected to teach. See "Teaching" for the complete calculation, and more information on how it is calculated.

  • Teaching Workload - This is the estimated teaching contribution for an individual (i.e. the ratio of their time spent on teaching responsibilities). This is calculated as hours / (1725 * fte).

  • Overall - This is the overall calculated workload, which is the calculated workload over the effective FTE.

See the workload report for more information on the calculation.
