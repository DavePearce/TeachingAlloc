Notes

  • Effective FTE - This is the full-time equivalent teaching load for an individual. It is considered "effective" because it is calculated from their contract FTE (e.g. part-time, full-time, etc), and any additional buyouts (e.g. from grants).

  • Research - This is the research contribution (i.e. the ratio of time spent on research activities) for a given staff member. Note, this does not include supervision, which is counted separately.

  • Teaching - This is the calculated teaching contribution, which is made up from two components: lecturing and supervision. See "Teaching" for the complete calculation, and more information on how it is calculated.

  • Admin - This is the admin contribution for an individual. See "Staff" for more information on this.

  • Overall - This is the overall calculated workload, which is the calculated workload over the effective FTE.
