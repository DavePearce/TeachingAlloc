Course Coverage Allocation

Legend

  • CC - Course Coordinator
  • NL - New Lecturer to course (gives additional load of 50%)
  • NC - New Course (gives additional load of 50%)
  • NS - New Staff member (gives additional load of 50%)

Notes

  • Hours - This is the calculated teaching hours. The hours for a course are calculated at 150 + n, where n is the number of enrolments. This is then distributed to staff based on their load for the course. Course Coordinators get a bonus 20%. Lecturers on new courses, or who are teaching a course for the first time, get an additional 0.50 bonus. Likewise, new staff are given a 0.5 bonus for their first two years (note: this differs slightly from the official workload model).
