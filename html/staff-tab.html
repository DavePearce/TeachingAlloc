Notes

  • Effective FTE - This is the full-time equivalent. It is considered "effective" because it is calculated from their contract FTE, and any additional buyouts (e.g. from grants).

  • Base Hours - This provides a "base" level of hours for each staff member. This is primarily used to factor in ENGR489 supervision at a rough cost of 50 hours per student.
