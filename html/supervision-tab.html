Staff Contribution Allocation (each student is worth 5%)

Notes

  • Contribution - This is the calculated supervision contribution. The normal teaching contribution is 50% (i.e. with 40% for research and 10% for admin) and is made up from two components: lecturing and supervision. A teaching contribution of 50% is equivalent to teaching four "standard" courses and, hence, each "standard" course gives a 12.5% teaching contribution. One "standard" course is equivalent to 2.5 post-graduate supervisions (PGS), with a maximum of 3.5 PGS being counted.
